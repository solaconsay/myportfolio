About Me

Who I am and what I do

"Bridging Networking, Automation, and Cybersecurity."

I’m a Senior Network Performance Analyst with over eight years of experience in IT, specializing in networking, automation, and data analysis. Throughout my career, I’ve been driven by a commitment to improving efficiency, solving complex problems, and delivering impactful solutions.

With a strong foundation in systems administration and a deep focus on performance optimization, I’ve led numerous projects that improved network reliability and operational workflows. My growing passion for cybersecurity and penetration testing has led me to proactively enhance security measures while streamlining processes through automation and AI integration.

I believe in staying ahead of the curve by continuously learning and adapting to the latest technologies. Whether it’s improving system performance, automating tasks, or fortifying defenses, I aim to make a meaningful impact in every role I take on.
